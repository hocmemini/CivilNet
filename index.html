Civil‑Net

Empower families & teams to anticipate, coordinate & thrive under disruption.

Civil‑Net is the first readiness SaaS that fuses live geopolitical signal feeds with role‑based logistics, turning complex crises into actionable checklists for households, SMEs, and affinity groups.

Clan Mobilization Blueprint

A scalable plan for family-based emergency response, organized by functional cells with clear triggers.

Functional Cells

  • Medical – triage SOPs, 30-day meds, secure records
  • Logistics – inventory stocks, assign depots, burn-rate forecasts
  • Mobility – rally routes, fuel caches, convoy SOP
  • Comms – Signal ➜ Matrix ➜ ham radio stack, key rotation
  • Finance – coop LLC, credit union accounts, aid agreements
  • Security – range days, standardized calibers, rules of engagement

Standing Triggers

  1. Green – maintain chat and inventories
  2. Yellow – 72‑h check-in, pre-stage essentials
  3. Orange – wire funds, load pallets, site security
  4. Red – phased relocation, 24/7 watch, nightly ham nets

Mountain West sites offer low target profile, strong logistics, and friendly laws. Initial steps: set up a secure cloud space, form the coop LLC, and schedule the first field day.
